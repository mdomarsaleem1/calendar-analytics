## Best Practices Recommendations
- **High Priority**: Excessive meeting time (>6 hrs/day); high calendar fragmentation; defaulting to 1-hour meetings.
- **Medium Priority**: Recurring meeting percentage >70%; low response/acceptance rates; vague meeting names.
- **Positive Patterns**: Healthy 1:1 ratio; strong cross-functional collaboration; consistent meeting lengths.
